This is me

Ruby Mus

Hi there!
 Ruby Mus, the stage name of Myrthe Robijns, takes you to another dimension with her fresh, cheerful melodies. Inspired by the color red, she translates complex emotions about love, conflict and self-empowerment through well-chosen words, harmonies, but also dance.

She creates a unique sound by combining her roots in R&B and soul music with modern ones electronic soundscapes, provided by the several producers working with her collaborate. Showing your emotions and feelings through words and dance are the purest ways to express yourself.
